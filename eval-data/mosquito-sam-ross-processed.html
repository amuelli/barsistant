Ingredients:
22.5 ml Del Maguey Vida Clásico Mezcal
22.5 ml Strucchi Red Bitter (Campari-style liqueur)
22.5 ml Lemon juice (freshly squeezed)
22.5 ml Ginger syrup (equal parts ginger juice to caster sugar by weight
3 drop Saline solution 4:1 (20g sea salt to 80g water)
× 1 1 serving
Read about cocktail measures and measuring

How to make:

  1. Select and pre-chill a Coupe glass.
  2. Prepare garnish of skewered crystallised ginger.
  3. SHAKE all ingredients with ice.
  4. FINE STRAIN into chilled glass.
  5. Garnish with skewered crystallised ginger.
